Antenna Tracking Station

Sep 2018 - Apr 2019
 Vancouver, BC
Led a team of 8 to develop an antenna tracking station. The station was programmed using Python on a Raspberry Pi to continuously orient itself towards the aircraft. Its open-loop control uses GPS data from the station and aircraft to continuously calculate the new heading. With 2DOF, the station can be manipulated in yaw and pitch directions. This project reduced flight mission time by 30% as it allowed long range live image transmission to the base station.
